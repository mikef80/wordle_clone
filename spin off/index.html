Welcome to Colourdle!


The object of the game/exercise is to guess the RGB value of the displayed colour swatch. First three columns are for the R(ed) value, next three are for the G(reen) value and last three are for the B(lue) value.


You must use leading zeros (for example, 053, not 53) else you'll break everything. At the end of the line, press or click enter to submit your answer. The rest should be fairly straight forward.
